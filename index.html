Eutawville, SC

Official Town Website

Warm wishes from the Town of Eutawville. We hope our website meets your needs as you conduct business, obtain information, or learn about our community. Eutawville is a peaceful town located on the banks of Lake Marion. We offer a lakeside life style with a wide variety of recreational activities and affordable housing stock.

Payment

  • You can pay court fines, traffic tickets, and water bills online using a major credit card (a small service fee will be charged)
  • Please contact the nCourt Client Support Hotline with any questions or concerns from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM Monday through Friday
  • For water bills and business licenses calls, call 877-794-1145
  • For court inquiries, call 833-881-3234
